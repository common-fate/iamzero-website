# Support

Help is available for all users through the Common Fate community Slack. You can [join the Slack channel here](https://join.slack.com/t/commonfatecommunity/shared_invite/zt-q4m96ypu-_gYlRWD3k5rIsaSsqP7QMg). Feel free to ask any questions or chat about any of your experiences with IAM Zero. We'd love to hear from you and there's a friendly community who can help.

If you're using IAM Zero at work, we'd love to help you with implementation support and training. Professional support governed by an SLA is available - please contact us at hello@commonfate.io if you would like to discuss.

[IAM Zero Enterprise](/enterprise) includes implementation and ongoing support.
